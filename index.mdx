title: "Introduction"
description: "Bonafi turns financial documents into structured, auditable truth."
---

Bonafi is a financial document platform for teams that ingest statements, notices, and reports — then extract, review, and commit structured facts with full provenance.

You upload a source file; the system parses it, proposes values, and routes uncertain mappings through a review queue. Approved proposals become truth: metrics, entities, and relationships your portfolio can query.
