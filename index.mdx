Bonafi is a financial document platform. Teams ingest statements, notices, and reports, the system parses them, proposes values, and routes uncertain mappings through review. Approved proposals become truth: metrics, entities, and relationships your portfolio can query.

## How we operate

We move quick and accurate. Every tool sits behind a seam, so we pivot to anything better in no time: LlamaParse parses today, Reducto is on the radar, same contract either way. Found something faster or better? Say it. Pro-activeness is expected, not appreciated.
